My Story

Hello! I'm MaryAnn Nwagor, a passionate web developer with over 5 years of experience in creating digital experiences that make a difference. My journey into web development started during my computer science studies, where I discovered my love for turning ideas into interactive, beautiful websites.

I specialize in front-end development with a strong focus on user experience and responsive design. My goal is to create websites that not only look great but also provide seamless functionality across all devices.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or enjoying a good cup of coffee while reading about the latest web development trends.
